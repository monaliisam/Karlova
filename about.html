About Us

Welcome to Maldena, a vibrant and historic neighborhood located in the heart of Tartu. We are proud to showcase the rich culture and history of our city to visitors from around the world.

In Maldena, we believe in celebrating the past while embracing the future. Our neighborhood is home to stunning architecture, museums, and cultural landmarks, as well as a thriving culinary scene and vibrant nightlife. We are passionate about sharing the best of our neghborhood with visitors, and our people are dedicated to providing top-notch hospitality and customer service.

Whether you are visiting Maldena for the first time or you are a returning visitor, we hope you will take the time to explore our neghborhood and discover all that it has to offer. Thank you for choosing Maldena as your destination, and we look forward to welcoming you soon.
